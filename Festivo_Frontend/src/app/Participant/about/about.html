Festivo bridges the gap between passionate students and organizing institutions. We eliminate the chaos of manual registrations, scattered announcements, and disconnected results.

Whether you're a college hosting a massive tech summit, a coordinator managing multiple events, or a participant eager to showcase your talents, Festivo brings everything into one unified, breathtaking platform.
